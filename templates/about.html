Training Platform

A smarter way to bring discipline and structure to training.

Built for coaches

The platform is designed to reduce manual work and create a more consistent training experience from the first check-in to the final drill.

About Product

Built for real training sessions

TrainEdge is designed for cricket coaches and academies who want better discipline and smoother session flow. It replaces manual tracking with a simple system for attendance, queue management, and drill timing.

Features

What it handles

  • Attendance tracking with timestamps
  • Batting queue management
  • Drill and session timers
  • Late arrival tracking
